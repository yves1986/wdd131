Website Planning Document

Mindful Moments - Mindfulness & Meditation Resource Site

Site Name

Mindful Moments

This name was chosen because it reflects the core purpose of the website: helping users find moments of peace, mindfulness, and reflection in their daily lives. "Mindful" represents the practice of being present and aware, while "Moments" emphasizes that even small, brief practices can have a significant impact on mental well-being. The name is memorable, calming, and clearly communicates the site's focus on mindfulness and meditation.

Optional domain availability: mindful-moments.org

Site Purpose

The site provides a comprehensive resource for mindfulness and meditation practices. It offers guided breathing exercises, reflection prompts, and listing activities to help users reduce stress, increase self-awareness, and develop a regular mindfulness practice. The site also includes educational content about the benefits of mindfulness and a progress tracking feature to help users stay motivated.

Scenarios

  • What are some quick mindfulness exercises I can do during a busy workday?
  • How can I track my meditation progress and see how often I practice?
  • Where can I find guided breathing exercises for anxiety relief?

Color Schema

#2C5F2D (Forest Green) - Used for headings, navigation bar background, and footer background. Represents growth, calm, and nature.

#97BC62 (Sage Green) - Used for secondary headings, accent borders, and button backgrounds on hover.

#F5F5DC (Beige) - Used for body background color to create a warm, calming feel.

#4A4A4A (Dark Gray) - Used for body text for readability and contrast.

Typography

  • Montserrat (700, 600) - Used for all headings (h1, h2, h3). This sans-serif font is clean, modern, and easy to read.
  • Open Sans (400, 600) - Used for body text, paragraphs, and navigation links. This font is highly readable on screens and complements Montserrat well.
