Côte d'Ivoire (Ivory Coast)

Côte d'Ivoire is a country located in West Africa. It is the world's largest producer and exporter of cocoa beans. The country is also known for its vibrant culture, music, and dance. The economic capital is Abidjan, one of the most populous French-speaking cities in Africa. Côte d'Ivoire is home to over 60 ethnic groups, and its official language is French.
